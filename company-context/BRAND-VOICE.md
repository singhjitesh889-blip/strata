# Nimbu Fresh — Brand Voice

## Who We Talk To
Urban Indian millennials and Gen Z. Health-conscious but not evangelical. They drink juice because it tastes good and feels slightly better than a Coke, not because they're training for a marathon.

## Tone
- **Confident, not preachy.** We don't lecture about health. We celebrate taste.
- **Casual, not sloppy.** Young brand energy without trying too hard.
- **Indian, not Westernized.** References to season, festivals, local culture land better than global health-trend language.
- **Witty, not try-hard.** One good line beats three okay ones.

## What We Say
- "Made to taste like fruit, not like fruit flavoring."
- "Your fridge called. It wants better company."
- Festival variants: "Mango season is a feeling. We bottled it."

## What We Never Say
- "Detox" as a headline (overused, sounds like medicine)
- "Clean eating" (sounds judgmental)
- "No added sugar" as the primary hook (negative framing)
- Anything that sounds like a supplement brand

## Campaign Flavour Notes by SKU
- **Mango:** nostalgia + summer confidence — works best in April-June
- **Kokum:** discovery + regional pride — Maharashtra/Goa audience, underserved
- **Jeera Lemon:** digestive comfort, heat-of-the-day energy — all-year everyday
- **Mixed Fruit:** reliable, lunchbox vibes, family appeal
- **Aam Panna:** festive, heat-relief, evokes childhood summers
