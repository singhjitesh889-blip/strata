# Nimbu Fresh — Scenario Brief

## Who You Are
You are the new Growth Lead at Nimbu Fresh, a D2C juice startup based in Bangalore. The previous Growth Lead quit last Friday — personal reasons, no handoff. You start Monday.

## The Business
- **Product:** Cold-pressed and blended fruit juices in 300ml and 1L bottles
- **Channels:** Direct website + Amazon + quick commerce (Blinkit, Zepto)
- **Primary market:** Tier 1 cities — Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad
- **Positioning:** Fresher than Tropicana, more accessible than Raw Pressery
- **Price:** ₹45–85 per 300ml bottle, ₹150–220 for 1L

## The Problem
The last three months have been inconsistent. Some campaigns worked well, others bombed. Nobody documented why. The ops team has been keeping a running text log (`ops-log-june.txt`) but nobody senior has read it in weeks. There's a GST liability file that's been sitting untouched since February. The founder wants "one number" by Monday morning.

## The Founder
Vikram Bhatnagar. IIT Delhi → BCG → started Nimbu Fresh in 2022. Brilliant strategist, terrible at sitting still. He texts ROAS numbers at 11pm and expects answers by 9am. He will not open a dashboard. He will open a slide or a Telegram message.

## Your Mandate
Figure out what happened. What worked. What didn't. Why. And have a clean answer ready before Monday's 10am call.
