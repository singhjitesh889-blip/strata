# Module 06 — Verify Everything

**~1.5 hours · 3 lessons**

---

## What this module teaches

The highest-leverage habit in this course.

Give Claude a way to check its own work. That single change produces 2-3x better quality on anything involving data or analysis.

This module teaches the full verification loop — not just "run a skeptical reviewer," but the complete 5-step system: Task → Execute → Eval gate → Ground-truth → Commit on PASS.

---

## Boris's #1 tip

Every lesson in this module builds toward one insight:

> "The eval gate is the step that separates AI that sounds right from AI that actually is right."

Vikram won't fact-check your founder update before his 10am call. Your skeptical-cfo will — if you ask it to.
