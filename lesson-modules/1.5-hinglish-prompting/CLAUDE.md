## What Actually Matters in a Prompt

Three things that DO change output quality:

**1. Specificity**
- Weak: "tell me about campaigns"
- Strong: "which 3 campaigns had ROAS above 8x in June, and what was different about them"

**2. Context**
- Weak: [just ask a question with no background]
- Strong: [give Claude the files + the question together]

**3. Format**
- "give me a table" vs "give me a paragraph" are genuinely different requests
- If you want bullet points, say so. If you want a slide-ready format, say that.
