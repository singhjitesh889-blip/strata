## How This Course Works

This isn't a video course. There are no slides. There's no "pause and rewind."

Everything in this course happens right here — you type, I respond, we work through it together. Learning by doing, not watching.

Every lesson has STOP markers where I wait for you. Don't skip them — the learning is in the doing, not the reading.

## The Scenario

You're the new Growth Lead at **Nimbu Fresh** — a D2C juice startup based in Bangalore.

The previous Growth Lead quit last Friday. No handoff. No documentation. Just a folder called `inherited-chaos/` and a founder who texts at 11pm.

Your job: figure out what happened, what worked, what didn't, and have a clean answer ready before Monday's 10am call with Vikram — the founder.

This is perfect for learning Claude Code because you'll do a bit of everything: digging through files, parsing chaos, building systems, and eventually automating the whole thing.
