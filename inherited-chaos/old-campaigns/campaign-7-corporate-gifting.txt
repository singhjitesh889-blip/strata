CAMPAIGN BRIEF — CORPORATE GIFTING PUSH
Date: June 2026 | Owner: Previous Growth Lead | Status: Wrong channel

OBJECTIVE: Acquire corporate gifting clients (HR managers, office admins buying for teams).

CREATIVE: "Gift Fresh. Your team deserves Nimbu Fresh." Gift boxes imagery.

CHANNEL: Meta (Instagram/Facebook)

BUDGET: Rs 42,000

RESULTS: Irregular. A few large orders (B2B conversion model different from B2C). ROAS math doesn't apply the same way.

POST-MORTEM: Corporate buyers don't browse Instagram and impulse-buy juice for 200 employees. They:
1. Search Google for "corporate gift ideas Bangalore"
2. Get vendor referrals from other HR managers
3. Respond to LinkedIn outreach
4. Call directly after visiting website

Meta is the wrong channel for B2B. Should have been: Google Ads (search), LinkedIn, and a dedicated inbound landing page for corporate inquiries. The 5-6 orders that did come in (from Ops Log) were likely from people who saw the ad AND already had a gifting need — not because Meta drove them to buy.

LESSON: Channel-audience fit. B2B decisions need B2B channels.
