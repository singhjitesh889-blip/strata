CAMPAIGN BRIEF — EID GIFTING PUSH
Date: June 2026 | Owner: Previous Growth Lead | Status: Underperformed

OBJECTIVE: Drive gifting purchases around Eid al-Adha 2026 (June 27).

CREATIVE: "Share the freshness this Eid." Bottle gift sets. Warm color palette.

BUDGET: Rs 45,000

CAMPAIGN LIVE: June 24.

EID DATE: June 27.

GAP: 3 days of lead time. That's it.

RESULTS: Very poor. 2.1x ROAS. Below breakeven.

POST-MORTEM: Gifting campaigns need 2-3 weeks of runway minimum. Customer needs to see it, think about it, order it, and have it delivered before the occasion. 3-day window = zero chance. Basic calendar error. For August 15 (Independence Day) and future festivals — plan 3 weeks ahead minimum.

LESSON: Festival calendar planning is its own workstream. Needs to happen in advance, not the week of.
