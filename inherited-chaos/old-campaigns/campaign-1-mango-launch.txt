CAMPAIGN BRIEF — MANGO FRESH LAUNCH
Quarter: Q1 FY26-27 | Owner: Previous Growth Lead | Status: Completed

OBJECTIVE: Launch Mango Fresh as the hero SKU for summer 2026. Build awareness + drive first purchase.

TARGET AUDIENCE: Urban 22–35, Bangalore/Mumbai/Delhi, health-conscious occasional health purchasers.

CREATIVE DIRECTION: Vibrant yellow-orange palette. "This summer, drink like a mango." UGC-style video with real juice being poured. No stock footage.

CHANNELS: Meta (Reels + Stories primary), Google Display secondary.

BUDGET: Rs 1.2L for June (split 70/30 Reels vs Static)

RESULTS (preliminary): ROAS 8.4x average across June. Best single day: June 7, 9.2x (reason unknown — check Reels creative v2 boost).

WHAT WORKED: Reels format far outperformed static. "Squeeze fresh" hook in first 2 seconds correlated with spike.

WHAT DIDN'T: Static carousel ads underperformed. Desktop audience underperformed vs mobile.

NOTES: Strong campaign. Execution was stronger than briefing. Actual creative was developed in-house, brief was rough. Document the winning creative logic before it's forgotten.
