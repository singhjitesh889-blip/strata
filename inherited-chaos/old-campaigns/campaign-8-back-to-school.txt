CAMPAIGN BRIEF — BACK TO SCHOOL
Date: June 2026 | Owner: Previous Growth Lead | Status: Wrong audience

OBJECTIVE: Drive juice purchases from parents for school lunchboxes.

CREATIVE: "Fresh in the lunchbox. Nimbu Fresh." Kids' school imagery.

BUDGET: Rs 35,000

RESULTS: 2.8x ROAS. Weak.

POST-MORTEM: Nimbu Fresh is not positioned as a kids' brand. Our packaging is adult-first. Our product (Jeera Lemon, Kokum) is not in the "my child will drink this" mental category for parents. Mango might work but our creative did not feature Mango specifically.

Also: back-to-school in India is July/August, not June. June campaign was too early for the occasion.

LESSON: Occasion relevance + product-occasion fit both need to be right. Not just one.
