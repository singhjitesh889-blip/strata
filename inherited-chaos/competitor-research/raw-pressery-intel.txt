COMPETITOR INTEL — RAW PRESSERY
Category: Cold-pressed premium juice
Last updated: June 2026

POSITIONING: Health first. Cold-pressed = maximum nutrition. Premium urban health-conscious buyer. "Pressed. Never heated."

PRODUCTS: Cold-pressed single fruits + blends. Immunity shots. Cleanses. Subscription boxes.

PRICE: Rs 150–250 per bottle. Premium segment.

DISTRIBUTION: Primarily metro. Strong in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi. Quick commerce (Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart). Some D2C direct.

MARGINS: High per-unit but production cost is also high (cold-press equipment + short shelf life = logistics complexity).

MARKETING: Instagram-heavy. Health influencer partnerships. "Clean ingredient" messaging. Minimalist aesthetic.

RELEVANCE TO NIMBU FRESH:
- Price positioning gap: RP is Rs 150–250, we're Rs 50–85. Different buyer.
- RP buyer = aspiring health optimizer. Our buyer = health-aware but practical.
- Opportunity: buyers who want fresher than Tropicana but can't afford Raw Pressery consistently. That's our sweet spot.
- Don't try to out-premium Raw Pressery. Own the "fresh and accessible" middle.
