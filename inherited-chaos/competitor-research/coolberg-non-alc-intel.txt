COMPETITOR INTEL — COOLBERG
Category: Non-alcoholic beer / adult beverages
Last updated: June 2026

POSITIONING: Occasion-led. "The drink for every occasion." Social drinking without alcohol. Adult positioning, not health positioning.

PRODUCTS: Non-alcoholic beer in several flavors. Mint, Cranberry, Peach, Ginger, etc.

PRICE: Rs 70–100 per bottle. Premium for the category.

DISTRIBUTION: Modern trade, quick commerce, some D2C. Growing presence in corporate offices, events.

MARKETING: Occasion marketing. Office parties, Dry January, events where alcohol is restricted. Social/aspirational.

WHY THIS IS ON OUR RADAR:
- Adjacent category — same usage occasion (social drinks, something "interesting" that isn't just water or Coke)
- They've cracked a different positioning (fun + adult + no alcohol) that we haven't tried
- Their rapid growth shows there's appetite for non-standard adult beverages in India
- Some Kokum/Aam Panna variants could be positioned similarly as "interesting" adult choices

LESSON FROM COOLBERG:
- Occasion marketing works for beverages when alcohol-free occasions are framed positively
- "What you drink at a party" is a real decision category
- Nimbu Fresh could own "the healthy choice at the office" if positioned right
